The recipe of Pastisseria Palmira

Syria Sweet and Mor

Ingredients

  • Pistachio
  • Margarine
  • Knava
  • Diameter

steps of preparation

  1. We extend the Layer of Knava on a Plate
  2. Put the Pistachio
  3. Extend the 2 Layer of Knava above Pistachio
  4. Put to much of Margarine
  5. Put the Knava in the Oven for half an hour at 180 c
  6. Then put the Diameter on it
